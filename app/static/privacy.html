Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Introduction

Zen Labs provides an AI-powered customer service assistant for Facebook Page owners. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information we collect

  • Facebook Page data — page name, page ID, and access tokens to operate the chatbot.
  • Messages — messages sent to your page via Messenger, used to generate AI responses.
  • Order information — customer name, phone, address, and payment method when orders are placed.
  • Products — product details from your page posts.

How we use it

To provide automated replies, process orders, display products, and send you order notifications. Nothing else.

Storage and security

Data is stored in encrypted databases. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

Third parties

We use Meta (Facebook) for Messenger integration and AI services for generating responses. No personal data is retained by AI providers.

Your rights

You can access, modify, or delete your data by contacting support@zenlabs.app.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.
