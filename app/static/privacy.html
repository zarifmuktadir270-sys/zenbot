Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Introduction

ZenBot ("we", "our", "us") provides an AI-powered customer service assistant for Facebook Page owners. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we may collect:

  • Facebook Page Information: Page name, Page ID, and page access tokens to operate the chatbot on your behalf.
  • Customer Messages: Messages sent to your Facebook Page via Messenger, to generate AI responses.
  • Order Information: Customer name, phone number, delivery address, and payment method when customers place orders through the chatbot.
  • Product Information: Product details scraped from your Facebook Page posts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide automated AI responses to your customers on Messenger.
  • To process and track customer orders.
  • To display your product catalog to customers.
  • To send order notifications to you (the page owner).

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted databases. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Access tokens are stored securely and used only to operate the chatbot service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Meta/Facebook: For Messenger integration and page data.
  • AI Services: To generate intelligent responses (no personal data is stored by AI providers).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: support@zenbot.app

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
